Children Now

2024 California Children's Report Card

Overview

Children Now releases their biennial report card at the beginning of the year to provide research insights on children's wellbeing to policymakers and child-centric organizations in California.

My role in this project was to spearhead the visual design, including layout, typography, and color. Additionally, we created over 100 data visualizations in collaboration with the Director of Service Design and Senior Managing Director of Research.

All data visuals displayed here were designed by me.

view report →

Outcome

The report has been downloaded 3140 times to date (July 2024) and a higher demand for printed versions took us by surprise a couple months after the initial release in January 2024. This has made the report card a resounding success!

The report inspired a brand refresh due to release by the end of the year that I have been privileged to lead.
