Hello, I'm José Murillo 👋

I’m a design generalist with a proclivity for big-picture thinking and a fascination with human ingenuity. I care deeply about the well-being of individuals, society, and the natural world and I wish to direct my work towards the creation of experiences that promote thriving.

My career path into design has not been straightforward. My working experience is in customer service where I’ve learned a lot about human behavior and about what goes into creating positive experiences for others.

While working, I attended community college for seven years exploring my interests in psychology, media studies, computer science and cognitive science until arriving to UC Davis to study design. I graduated in September 2021.

Outside of design I enjoy running, reading, connecting over music, philosophical conversations, Magic the Gathering, and watching reality TV competitions.
