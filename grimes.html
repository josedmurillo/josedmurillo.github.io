GRIMES

An interactive web experience.

Overview

Combining my love for music and the web, I designed and developed an interactive website that shares a story about one of my favorite artists.

My roles and responsibilities included Creative Direction, Design, and Front-End Web Development.

This was a capstone project for my Interaction Design II course at UC Davis. I revisited the project in April 2023 to update visual elements, design for a responsive web, and fix bugs (thank you software engineer friend!).

view website →

Experience Goals

Grimes’ music, art and love of space inspired the visual design and functionality of the website. I wanted the look and feel to be like that of a spaceship's interface ready for exploration.

To accomplish this, I made the planets a central focal point with surface images that resemble each album cover.

The experience goal for the user was to encourage curiosity about Grimes using images, animations, and music interactions as I shared my own feelings and discovery of her work.
